    Our Mission

    Thrift stores help reduce textile waste by giving people a location to donate their unused clothes. The donated clothing is either sold in stores for people to purchase, recycled or exported to developing countries.

    About Thrift Fashion

    Thrift Fashion was Created around the idea of transparency. Buying used clothing wholesale means you are relying on the judgment of the team responsible for sorting it to guarantee that you are getting goods of the correct type and condition every time. That’s why everyone on the thrift fashion team from owners to the newest members- have to be big believers not only in the social responsibility of the work we do but also in our shared interest of openness, fair treatment and delivering consistent quality. Our values

    Loyal and Fair treatment of our client Unbiased and polite treatmrnt of our customers Professionalism in all our endeavors To work as a team to give Our potential Customers the best Positive thinking

    Sell With Us

    WE VALUE RE-USE

    We care about finding good homes for things and keeping them out of landfills. It's not just a transaction. We celebrate creative ways to reuse things around your home. It’s intrinsically satisfying to reuse things and be part of a community of others who reuse, working collectively toward a better planet and cleaner world.

    OUR TEAM

    A TEAM THAT'S EMPOWERED, DIVERSE AND INCLUSIVE

    We recognize that the quality of our team’s work directly affects the quality of work from teams that rely on our output, and this affects our customers’ experiences and perceptions. This will directly contribute to the success of our company by ensuring maximum profitability, minimal customer service incidents, and improving the effectiveness and efficiency of all dependent teams.

    Our Vision

    Thrift Fashion is to strive to be a global leader in fashion-knit and fashion outerwear by recycling used clothes and make used cloth available for the masses. Our Company to be a symbol of transparency, a team built up by owners sharing common interest to provide used cloth available for the masses.
